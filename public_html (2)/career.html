  • 🌟 Social Media Marketing Manager 🌟

    📋 Job Summary: IgniteAndRise is seeking a dynamic and creative Social Media Marketing Manager to lead our social media strategy, enhance our online presence, and drive engagement across multiple platforms. The ideal candidate will have a strong understanding of social media trends, content creation, and analytics, with a passion for building brand awareness and driving business growth through social media channels. This role also includes responsibilities for graphic design and managing the company’s social media pages. 🎯 Key Responsibilities: 📈 Social Media Strategy: Develop and implement a comprehensive social media strategy aligned with the company’s marketing goals and objectives. ✍️ Content Creation: Create, curate, and manage engaging content (text, images, videos, etc.) for all social media platforms, ensuring brand consistency and message alignment. 🎨 Graphic Design: Design visually appealing graphics, including social media posts, banners, posters, and reels, that align with the brand's identity and resonate with the target audience. 🎥 Reels and Video Content: Produce and edit engaging video content, including reels and short-form videos, optimized for social media platforms. 📱 Page Management: Manage and maintain the company’s social media pages, ensuring regular updates, consistent branding, and active engagement with followers. 📊 Campaign Management: Plan and execute social media campaigns, including paid advertising, to drive brand awareness, website traffic, and lead generation. 👥 Community Management: Monitor, respond to, and engage with followers on social media platforms to build a strong online community and foster brand loyalty. 📈 Analytics and Reporting: Track, analyze, and report on social media performance metrics, using insights to optimize strategies and improve engagement. 🔍 Trend Analysis: Stay updated on the latest social media trends, tools, and best practices, and apply this knowledge to enhance the company’s social media presence. 🤝 Collaboration: Work closely with the marketing team, content creators, and designers to ensure cohesive messaging and branding across all channels. 🔗 Influencer Marketing: Identify and collaborate with influencers and brand advocates to amplify the company’s reach and impact on social media. 👂 Social Listening: Monitor online conversations and brand mentions, addressing any issues or opportunities to enhance the company’s reputation. 🎓 Qualifications: 🎓 Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Communications, Graphic Design, or a related field. 📝 Proven experience (3+ years) as a Social Media Manager, Graphic Designer, or similar role.
